# About Me

I have been blogging on and off since 1999 (more off at this point) but I'm hoping that with the
general decline of social media maybe it'll stick around this time. When I'm not writing posts for
this site or swimming in my vast Steam backlog I'm also [a game developer][3]; in my day job, I'm a full
stack developer instead. But I'm not very good at design, as anyone can tell by looking at this site;
graphic design is _not_ my passion.

You can find me on [Bluesky][1], or on [Away Message Club][2], the small Mastodon instance I am admin
for.
